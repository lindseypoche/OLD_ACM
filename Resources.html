Kattis Training

Kattis is a great resource for both practicing your skills with a programming language you are familiar with as well as a tool to pick up a new language. The style of problems are also great to prepare for competitive programming competitions. If this is your goal, try a range of problems from difficulty 1.2 to 6 or 7 to best prepare.
